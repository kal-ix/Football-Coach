Data & Account Deletion

Football Coach · Instructions for data deletion

What data you can delete

Football Coach allows you to delete your data in several ways, depending on how you use the app.

1. Delete your account and all cloud data (from the app)

If you have signed in with Google, you can delete your account and all associated cloud data directly from the app.

Steps:

  1. Open the Football Coach app
  2. Tap the side menu icon (☰) in the top left corner
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete account and cloud data
  4. Read the warning carefully and confirm by tapping "Yes, delete everything"
  5. If prompted, sign in again with Google to verify your identity

What gets deleted:

  • ✅ Your Firebase Authentication account (Google sign-in for the app)
  • ✅ Your user profile from the cloud database (name, email, profile photo)
  • ✅ All teams you own, including:
    • Players (personal data and photos)
    • Training sessions (sessions and exercises)
    • Matches (results, lineups, statistics)
  • ✅ Your membership in shared teams (you will be removed as a member)
  • ✅ All pending invitations sent to your email address

What is NOT deleted:

  • ❌ Local data stored on your device (local Hive database). This data stays on your phone and you can continue using the app in offline mode.
  • ❌ Your Google account — it is only disconnected from the app, not deleted from Google.
  • ❌ Shared teams where you are NOT the owner — they continue to exist for other members.

⚠️ Warning: This operation is irreversible. Once confirmed, cloud data cannot be recovered.

2. Delete a single team from the cloud

If you want to remove only one team from the cloud without deleting your account:

  1. Open the team you want to remove
  2. Go to team settings
  3. Select Delete team

All cloud data for that team (players, training sessions, matches) will be deleted. Local data on the device can still be kept.

3. Leave a shared team

If you are a member of a team shared by another coach and you want to remove your access:

  1. Open the shared team
  2. Go to Manage Members
  3. Tap Leave Team

You will be removed from the members list and will no longer have access to the team data.

4. Sign out

If you simply want to stop synchronization without deleting any data:

  1. Open the side menu (☰)
  2. Tap Sign out

Synchronization will stop, but your data will remain in the cloud. You can resume syncing by signing in again.

5. Delete local data

To delete data stored locally on your device:

  • Uninstall the app from your device. All local data will be removed.
  • Alternatively, go to Phone Settings → Apps → Football Coach → Clear Data.

6. Request deletion via email

If you are unable to delete data from the app, or if you want written confirmation of deletion, you can contact us directly:

📧 request.kalix@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  • The email address of the Google account used in the app
  • The team name (if applicable)
  • Your specific request (e.g., "delete my account and all associated data")

We will respond and process the deletion within 30 days of the request, as required by GDPR and CCPA.

Summary
