Everything you need

A complete tool to manage every aspect of your coaching activity.

📋

Complete Squad Management

Create and manage multiple teams. Add players with photos and position. View individual stats: appearances, goals, assists, cards.

🏃

Plan Your Training

Create detailed sessions with exercises, images and descriptions. Record attendance and absences. Export training sheets to PDF.

⚽️

Match Management

Schedule matches, select squad members, create formations by dragging players on the pitch. Record results, scorers and cards. Create tactical schemes.

📊

Detailed Statistics

Minutes played, goals scored, assists made, yellow and red cards, training attendance. Everything at a glance.

📄

PDF Export

Generate professional sheets for players, training and matches. Easily share with staff and management with a clean design.

📚

Exercise Database

Library of customizable exercises with tactical images. Organize by category and objective. Import and export exercises in JSON format.

☁️

Cloud Synchronization

Sign in with Google and sync your team across multiple devices. Invite other coaches to collaborate with custom roles (owner, editor, viewer).

☕️

Team Sharing

Share your team with your staff via email. Every member sees real-time updated data. Manage access permissions.

✍️

Tactical Board

Draw tactical schemes with arrows, curves and shapes. Place players and equipment. Save and export your strategies as images.

📅

Event Calendar

Create custom events beyond training sessions and matches. View everything on an integrated calendar with date, location and notes.
